About my Self and its many delusions

Initially, I had indeeed momentarily thought of traversing from Sagan to Nietzche through Sartre to put some profound quotes in the pretense of describing myself. :P
but I have now long come to terms with the holistic futility of any existence in the grander scheme of everything in this universe that I have tried to study.
but since it's still necessary...

My confusion with my own existence had gifted me a place at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur as an MS(by Research) student at their Cognitive Science program.
I wont be able to put into words the experience it has been.

I had my undergraduate training as a B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering graduate from Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata.
The best I can say about it is that even in the haystack, I fortunately had stumbled upon some of the most extraordinary things and beings, who I think have changed everything for whom I am, the little I am, today.

Probably I define myself by putting out in my first breath that I didn't have a good home. But I got to experience atleast the taste of a better childhood and adolescence (Grades 1 to 12) at my second home, South Point High School, Kolkata
